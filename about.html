Who we are.

and What we do

Our mission with the University of Manitoba Chess Club is to provide a relaxed and social environment for chess players of all levels to gather and play chess. The Club will hold regular sessions, tournaments, as well as social events away from the board.

Membership is FREE and will be for the foreseeable future (if possible FOREVER) and everyone is welcome to join through the sign up link. SIGNUP HERE.
